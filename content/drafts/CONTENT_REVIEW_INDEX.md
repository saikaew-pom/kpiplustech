## Review checklist

- Confirm company naming and final brand terminology.
- Have Thai legal or privacy counsel review PDPA, TM30, tax, and regulated statements before publication.
- Have fluent editors review Thai templates and future localized versions.
- Confirm product capabilities before converting educational language into product claims.
- Replace each local `featured_image_path` with its final R2 `featured_image_key` during CMS insertion.
- Schedule regulatory and platform-content reviews at least every six months.
